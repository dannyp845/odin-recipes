Cheeseburger Recipe

How to Make

Here's how to make an American classic.

Just follow these simple steps.

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns
  • Burger Patty
  • Mayo
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Grill the burger patty for 5 minutes on each side
  2. When finished, spread mayo on the burger buns
  3. Place the cooked patty in between the buns
  4. Add melted cheese on top
  5. Add condiments
  6. Add pickles
  7. Add onions, lettuce, and tomatoes
