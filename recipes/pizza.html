Pizza Recipe

How to Make

Here's how to make an all time classic.

Follow these simple steps.

Ingredients

  • Dough for crust
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Nead the dough into a ball
  2. Spread the dough in a circular motion
  3. Generously apply pizza sauce around the dough
  4. Sprinkle mozzerella cheese on top of the sauce
  5. Add pepperoni as desired
  6. Season with garlic around the edge of the crust
