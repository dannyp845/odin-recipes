Lasagna Recipe

How to Make

Here's how to make one of the best dishes out there following one of my favorite recipes.

This recipe is made to serve 6-10 people.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes

Steps

  1. In a Dutch oven, cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium head until well browned.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiler water.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake additional 25 minutes.
